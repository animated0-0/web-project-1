Valorant

Valorant is one of my favorite games. Valorant is a first person shooter tactical hero shooter video game developed and published by Riot Games. Valorant takes inspiration from the Counter-Strike series, borrowing several mechanics such as the buy menu, spray patterns, and inaccuracy while moving.

Agent Ability Picture
Neon Neon is a Duelist in Valorant, and she excels at speed, agility, and aggressive playstyles. Her abilities revolve around speed boosts, fast mobility.
Reyna Reyna is a Duelist in Valorant, known for her self-sustain and high individual carry potential. Her abilities revolve around healing, becoming invulnerable.
Jett Jett is a Duelist in Valorant, known for her agility, evasiveness, and high mobility. She excels at outmaneuvering enemies and disrupting their positioning with her speed.
